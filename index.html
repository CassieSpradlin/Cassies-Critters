About Me

Growing up, my mom taught me to crochet and I'd occassionally make a scarf here and there. In 2018 I had to have ankle surgery which gave me a lot of free time. I decided to pick up crochetting again and was looking for a challenge. I started small with learning new stitches for dishcloths and towels and made a few blankets for Christmas that year. After I was able to conquer those challenges, I saw the cutest little stuffed bunny someone had crafted for Easter. I decided to try it out and after watching many videos and some helpful hints from friends, I was able to get it completed and kept improving my skills with every critter. I love making all sorts of crochet creations, especially for family and friends.
