Sleepy Unicorn

Last year I tried my hand at my very first teddy bear. It was for a friend's daughter who absolutely loves stuffed animals, so I couldn't disappoint. Recently I had posted another finished critter on my Facebook page, she saw it and needed another animal for her daughter's birthday. Even with as beginner as the bear was, she apparently loved it and hadn't put it down.

Pink was a favorite color, so the critter color was a given. She searched and found the cutest monkey by a designer who has a whole safari of critters (literally). I was excited for the challenge and got to work.
